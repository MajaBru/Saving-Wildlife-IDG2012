Be the voice of the animals. Save the norwegian wildlife.

Each and every wild being deserves to be met with respect and acceptance by humans. In reality, this is not the case. Therefore, we must dare to acknowledge and value the wildlife that surrounds us. Saving Wildlife is an organized movement fighting to give the rights back to the animals. More than 50,000 members nationwide give direct support to what we stand for and spreads the word.

Read about our work!

We must act now

Worldwide, wild animals make up only 4% of the biomass of all mammals - humans make up 36% and livestock in the meat industry 60%. As for birds, the wild birds make up 30%. The UN warns that we are facing a man-made mass extinction. Despite this, in Norway we see increased exploitation of and less tolerance for wild animals.
